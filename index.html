Hype o'clock

Game Dev & Tea Time

Hype o'clock is a casual co-working meetup in the San Francisco Bay Area. We meet the third Saturday of every month to work on and show off projects, drink tea, and socialize.

A bunch of cool stuff has been made at Hype o'clock, including itch.io, Frog Fractions 2, Teacart 1k, and the GDC Art Jam party.

The event is currently hosted at a private residence in Oakland with capacity for approximately 20 people. For location, please ask on our Discord or email info@hypeoclock.org. We also have a Meetup page.
